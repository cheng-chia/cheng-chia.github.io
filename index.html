About Me

My name is Cheng-Chia Huang. I am a graduate student in San Diego State University, having five-year geography teaching experience. My current works focus on human dynamics and analysis of geographic information in social media.

I have got my first Master degree in National Taiwan University, specializng in cultural geography and geography education. Now, I am persuing my second Master degree in GIScience. My research interests are GIS, analysis of big geo-data, image classification, and topic classification.
