This project creates a tool written in PostgreSQL and Python, which is able to replicate gentrification typologies based on five exiting census-based gentrification typology strategies.

The five census-based gentrification typology strategies used in this project are (1) Bostic and Martin's method, (2) Freeman's method, (3) Ding et al.'s method, (4) Mckinnish et al.'s method, and (5) Voorhees Center's method. The maps below are the visualized results in Salt Lake City, UT.
