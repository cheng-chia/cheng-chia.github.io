This product, a dashboard monitoring mental health status in San Diego area, was developed by Cheng-Chia Huang, Joey Lee, and Zhengcong Yin during 2017 San Diego Big Data Hackathon and won the Most Thorough Design and Development Award. This product provides mental health information and analyzes the percentage of positive twites and negative twites in San Diego County.

The web map is based on ArcGIS API for JavaScript, and the sentiment analysis is based on Natural Language Toolkit. The data used were retrieved from Livewell and Twiter API.
